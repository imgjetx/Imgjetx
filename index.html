Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images safe with ImgJetX?

Absolutely. ImgJetX processes everything strictly on the client side. Since your files are never transmitted to external servers, your data privacy remains completely uncompromised. You can even run the entire process entirely offline.

Will compression ruin my image quality?

No. Our advanced compression engine optimizes structural file metadata to strip out unnecessary weight while maintaining high visual clarity. You can also activate the built-in JetImage HDR Enhancer (🌟) to dynamically upscale clarity, sharpness, and colors.

What are the bulk uploading size limits?

You can upload a batch queue of up to 10 images concurrently. For optimal browser safety, we enforce a strict single-file threshold of 30MB and an absolute cumulative batch restriction of 300MB to avoid sudden hardware memory lags.

Can I bundle my processed photos into a PDF?

Yes, effortlessly! ImgJetX supports exporting batches to standard document layouts. You can combine multiple processed image formats directly into a single unified PDF layout, or instantly package standalone multi-page documents seamlessly within an immediate automated ZIP folder wrapper.
